## What it does

Fabric Toolbox is a repository from the Microsoft Fabric Customer Advisory Team (Fabric CAT) collecting tools, accelerators, scripts, and samples that speed up adoption of Microsoft Fabric. It includes monitoring solutions (such as cost analysis and admin/platform monitoring, including FUAM), accelerators for backup/recovery and CI/CD, database scripts, and assessment and migration tooling.

## Why use it

Treat it as a curated grab-bag of field-tested Fabric assets: start from a proven accelerator or script rather than building common platform tasks from scratch.

## Getting started

Browse the repository at https://aka.ms/FabricToolbox and open the folder (monitoring, accelerators, samples, scripts, or tools) that matches your need.
